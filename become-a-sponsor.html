Why becoming a sponsor?

The partnership of such an event gives you the opportunity to have direct contact with young consumers and allows you to enhance your company by having a close link with them. This is why the effectiveness of this type of sponsorship is greater than that of conventional advertising, which is often more expensive and less effective. This event will bring together more than 200 people and will benefit from an important communication plan. Indeed, students will have the opportunity to meet and discuss. This is why, in view of the full success of our event, the presence of a company of such scale that yours is essential to us. By becoming a partner, you enhance your products and your benefits during the hackathon.
